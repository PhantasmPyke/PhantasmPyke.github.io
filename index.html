Coaching

Coaching is a process where you share your screen and recieve instant feedback and optimal actions to take. At the end of the game we will hold a discussion on how you can improve.

02
Duo/Duoing

Duoing in games means that we play together and I take the iniciative of carrying the game(winning) it for you. Whenever you achieve the desired amount of wins we end the Duo.

03
Boosting

Boosting is a process where you provide me of the log in information for your account and i achieve the desired amount of wins instead of you. This service is for premium clients.
